Welcome to the Let's Do Digital Clinic

Welcome to Let's Do Digital Clinic! While the name might suggest a traditional healthcare facility, we are not a real clinic. Instead, this is a website that is built by healthcare proffesionals, especially doctors, who are looking to learn front end engineering

At Let's Do Digital Clinic, we understand that technology is playing an increasingly important role in healthcare. From telemedicine to electronic health records, the ability to understand and navigate digital tools is becoming just as essential as clinical expertise. However, many doctors and healthcare workers are not formally trained in coding.

By building this website, clinicians will get hands on experience in the crafting of the basic building blocks of front end development, creating a foundation that will allow them to expand forwards.
